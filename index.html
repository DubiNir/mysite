About Me

Software Quality Assurance
& Automation Engineer

Hi, my name is Nir Hadash, 36 years old from Kfar Saba currently. In recent years I have worked in the prison service. I was a "registrar", a position responsible for all administrative and operational aspects of inmate life. After several years in the position, I felt that I was not challenging my abilities to the fullest. I began to think about changing career paths.

I researched the QA profession. I found information online and through friends. Very quickly, I found myself drawn to a new and interesting field. I did a free online course, and learned terms and tools. A few months later, I started a QA course led by Kobi Yonasi and immediately after that a QA Automation course led by Gal Matlon at AUTOMATION COLLEGE - www.automation.co.il (excellent lecturers!).

These days I am following two courses in which I created projects from scratch, I expanded my knowledge and learned many topics such as: QA methodologies, types of tests, writing test documentation (STP, STD, STR), Java, SQL, HTML, CSS, XML, TestRail, JIRA , Selenium WebDriver, TestNG and more.
So if you're looking for someone, I'm sure you won't regret it if you contact me.
